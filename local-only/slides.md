### **Slide 3: Cultural Forces: "Defensive Driving"**
* **Slide Text (Visual):**
    * **Safety First:** Yielding as a spontaneous, community-driven habit.
    * **Defensive Driving:** Letting the pedestrian make the first move.
    * **Power Dynamics:** *"The driver has the greater power... you have to be more responsible with your action."*
* **Speaker Notes:**
    * "What we learned is that this behavior is deeply ingrained through something called 'defensive driving.' It's not just about avoiding tickets; it's a cultural mindset. Drivers are taught that because they are operating a heavy vehicle, they hold the power and therefore the ultimate responsibility to yield to the more vulnerable person on the road."
